The Self-Service Kiosk system provides its users with both convenience and utmost security. The Kiosk system is developed to cater to a wide spectrum of requirements ranging from visa or passport application submissions to registration of citizen cards. This self-service solution brings a greater flexibility and enhanced reach to client governments by enabling them to provide a multi-channel approach for application submissions.

Through VFS Global’s Kiosk technology, an applicant can independently complete an entire range of functions from submitting their applications and enrolling their biometrics, to opting for value-added services, completing payments and obtaining acknowledgment receipts.
