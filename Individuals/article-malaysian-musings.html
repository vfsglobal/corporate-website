• With the sun shining bright and strong, it is the season for a refreshing summer break to melt the heat away. When you are looking for a break that is fun, easy and relaxing why not head to Malaysia? It’s the perfect destination for a family getaway or even a solo holiday!

      Family Fun

      When in Malaysia, a must visit with the entire family is the LEGOLAND® Malaysia Resort where you can explore a plethora of options with interesting LEGO® elements. This is the only LEGO®-themed hotel in Southeast Asia where you can stay in your own pirate cabin deck, an adventure-themed room or a kingdom-featured royal family room or even in a ninja cave! There are several thrilling rides that will take you through twists and turns; a must-try is the world’s first LEGO® VR roller coaster. Head to the LEGO® Studio to catch a 4D movie with thrills and spills like no other or build your very own LEGO® raft with colourful LEGO® bricks and float through the water bodies nestled among the beauty of nature

      For some more adventure, head to the Sunway Lagoon whose Water Park, Amusement Park, Scream Park, Wildlife Park, Extreme Park and Nickelodeon Lost Lagoon are sure to set your adrenaline rushing. This apart, there are several performances, parades and character appearances taking place throughout the day that are sure to keep you and your family entertained. There are also live animal shows and interactive wild encounters where you can experience up-close interaction with the animals of the park.

      Asia’s first Nickelodeon-themed park – Nickelodeon Lost Lagoon is where you can meet characters like SpongeBob SquarePants, Dora the Explorer and the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. The place is also home to Malaysia’s largest sandy surf beach with waves that are about eight metres high.

    • Luxe Getaway

      Your holiday will be incomplete if you don’t find the perfect stay to soothe you and help you relax. In such a scenario, the Lexis Hibiscus Port Dickson, that overlooks Port Dickson’s Beach and the Straits of Malacca, offers brilliant and luxurious accommodation options with its 117 tower rooms and 522 overwater villas. Incidentally, this hotel has two world records for the “most number of swimming pools in a resort” and “most overwater villas in a single resort.”

      The resort is a destination in itself, courtesy an array of fun indoor activities that are available on site. Young children will enjoy themselves at Kidz World whose activities are designed to introduce them to new experiences. You can also unwind at LexSpa where the massages and beauty treatments are guaranteed to rejuvenate your body and mind or head to their Starz Karaoke where guests can sing their hearts out.

      Outdoor activities like bike tours, archery, helicopter tours are also available at the resort. Adjacent to the resort are also rows of street food style stalls that will ensure you get to experiment with and taste authentic Malaysian fare.

    • How to Reach

      The best way to reach Malaysia is to hop on to a flight aboard Malaysian Airlines. Its all-new A350 fleet will be a memorable experience for you and all. With great options on economy and business class and superb connectivity, your journey will be as fun and comfortable as the destination itself. So get ready for a Malaysian adventure this summer!
