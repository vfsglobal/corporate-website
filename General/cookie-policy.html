Cookie Policy

  • What are cookies?

    Cookies are files or pieces of information that may be stored on system or internet enabled devices of the User, when the User visits the website/s of VFS Global. VFS Global uses cookies to make the website/s simpler to use and to create more valuable experiences for you as user. VFS Global cannot identify the User personally from this information. Cookies in themselves do not identify the individual user, just the computer or mobile device you are using via a randomly generated identifying tag.

  • How VFS Global use cookies?

    To get the best from the VFS Global website, you must accept the cookies. VFS Global uses Cookies to give you a better experience. VFS Global website offers certain features that are only available through the use of cookies.

    • The cookies help website users and maintain their signed-in status.
    • Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted from website user’s hard drive at the end of a session when the applicant/user exits the website. However “Persistent cookies” remain in place across multiple visits to our websites.

    The User may encounter cookies from website/s of VFS Global or its affiliate/s, joint venture partner/s, agencies, subsidiaries and clients on some pages of the VFS Global websites that VFS Global does not control. The User should verify the type of cookies such affiliate/s, joint venture partner/s, agencies, subsidiaries and clients may place of users computer/internet enabled devices and use their judgement on use of such websites

    Once a User accesses the website/s of VFS Global, the User consents to use of cookies by VFS Global. If the User does not agree to use of cookies, the User should not use website/s of VFS Global. If the cookies are disabled, it may impact the User experience on website/s of VFS Global.

    You as User can choose to block or delete Cookies, but please, be aware that if the cookies are disabled, it may impact the User experience on website/s of VFS Global.
