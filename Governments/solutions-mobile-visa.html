The Mobile Visa Application Centre is a customised mobile facility, equipped with end-to-end technology to enrol biometrics and accept applications from remote locations. This solution particularly benefits applicants who may find it challenging to visit the nearest fixed Visa Application Centre, and wish to submit their application at a location more convenient to them.

Apart from enhanced convenience to applicants, the flexibility of the Mobile Application Centre service also provides client governments with the option to temporarily use this cost-effective solution to cater to any seasonal or location-specific demand.
