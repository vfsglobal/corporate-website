• Venice Biennale, Italy

      May 11 - November 24

      The 58th Exhibition is curated by Ralph Rugoff and will include artworks that reflect upon different existential aspects, key traditions, institutions and relationships of the “post-war order”.

    • Cricket World Cup, England and Wales

      May 30 - June 14

      The Cricket World will see the participation of leading cricket playing nations where India, England and Australia start off as favourites. The matches will see thrilling encounters between the bat and ball and is likely to keep cricket lovers occupied much of the time.

    • Fete de la Musique, Paris

      June 21

      A not so ordinary music festival, live bands, singers, amateur musicians, drummers, Djs, etc take over the city’s streets, expressing themselves through music and inviting the crowd to enjoy themselves.

    • Singapore Food Festival, Singapore

      June/July

      The month-long extravaganza will see connoisseurs of food heading to Singapore to try its local food while trying out international cuisines as varied as Japanese, French, and Indian. This festival showcases the multicultural heritage of Singapore.

    • Obon Festival, Japan

      August 13 - 15

      It is a festival that celebrates familial bonds as Chochin (paper) lanterns are hung to guide the spirits and Obon dances (bon odori) are performed. Families have reunions and visit the graves of their relatives and make food offerings at altars and temples.

    • Canadian Grand Prix, Montreal, Canada

      July 7 - 9

      An event for racing enthusiasts, it doubles up as an opportunity for all to explore Quebec and enjoy the Grand Prix and cheer for your favourites from the crowd.

    • Mostly Mozart Festival, New York, USA

      July 17 - August 10

      The Lincoln Centre hosts a supremely engaging performance that sees unforgettable performances by the summer orchestra as Mozart comes alive. Several masterpieces of the legend keep the audiences entertained throughout the festival.

    • Cat Convention, Los Angeles, USA

      June 29 - 30

      It is the largest convention that sees cat owners, vendors from cat-centric merchandise and of course cats come together for a major adoption drive.

    • Khareef Festival, Salalah, Oman

      July 15 - August 31

      This annual celebration of the Khareef season in the Dhofar region of Oman is much sought after and liked by locals and tourists alike. This festival sees a fervent celebration of nature and culture together.

    • Jerash Festival of Culture and Arts, Jerash, Jordan

      July 19 - August 3

      Featuring poetry, theatre, literature, and the arts, the festival on the Roman ruins of Jerash, this festival brings together the cultural face of Jordan to the forefront
