• In India, in a move to keep up with the needs and expectations of citizens, professionally-managed citizen service centres and first-of–its-kind doorstep delivery of civic services have been launched by various authorities with the common objective of bringing to residents improved service efficiency, greater accessibility and enhanced convenience to more streamlined civic services.

      From seeking information online or through dedicated customer support channels to the proficient expedition of digitalised procedures, citizens are ensured an enhanced customer experience from start to finish.

    • Doorstep Delivery of Civic Services in Delhi

      Residents of Delhi can now have a specialised representative visit them at their home for a wide range of civic services.

      Services available in: Delhi, India
      Services provided: ‘Mobile Sahayaks’ provide doorstep delivery of civic services in the areas of driving licenses, marriage certificates and water connection registration, among several others, at the resident’s location of choice.
      Authority: Government of Delhi
      Business Hours: 0800 hrs to 2200 hrs (7 days a week)
      Call: 1076

    • Citizen Facilitation Centre in Manipur, India

      A state-of-the-art Citizen Facilitation Centre in Imphal, Manipur, provides residents with a wide range of proficiently managed E-district services as well as a host of sub-services, all under one roof.

      Services provided: Over 30 services, which includes E-district services related to the revenue department and employment exchange and transport services, health services and visa facilitation services (through a drop-box facility for Estonia, Thailand and Italy).
      Authority: Department of Municipal Administration, Housing & Urban Development (MAHUD), Government of Manipur
      Business Hours: 0900 hrs to 1800 hrs (Monday to Saturday)
      Call: 0385–2422222/24
      Email: manipurcfc@vfsglobal.com
      Address: Citizen Facilitation Centre, Near 2nd MR Gate, Secured Office Building, Imphal East, Imphal – 795001, Manipur, India.

    • Pravasi Bhartiya & Kshetriya Pravasi Sahayata Kendras

      In an effort to better equip blue-collared migrants looking to live and work abroad, specialised information support channels and migrant support centres are run across the country to provide aspiring migrants with reliable advice and guidance.

      Locations: One ‘Pravasi Bhartiya Sahayata Kendra’ (PBSK) is operational in Delhi and five ‘Kshetriya Pravasi Sahayata Kendra’ (KPSK) centres are operational in Delhi, Chennai, Lucknow, Hyderabad and Kochi in India.
      Services provided: Support services on information related to safe emigration practices. Support is rendered through walk-ins or 24x7 helplines, which provide information on legal procedures and requirements, organised and humane migration procedures and advice on the risks involved in illegal migration, amongst other assistance. The services are provided by Protector General of Emigrants – Nodal Officers appointed by MEA to safeguard blue collar workers intending to work abroad.
      Ministry: Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) of India
      Business Hours: 1000 hrs to 1700 hrs (Monday to Saturday) (7 days a week)
      Call: 1800 11 3090 / +91 11 40503090 / +91 11 26885021 (24x7 helpline support)
      Email: helpline@mea.gov.in
      Visit: www.mea.gov.in/Images/attach/14_OWRC_and_helpline.pdf

    • Citizen Facilitation Centres in Pune, India

      Professionally managed civic services are delivered across Pune via a robust network of Citizen Facilitation Centres and well-trained staff.

      Locations: 39 Citizen Facilitation Centres in all wards of Pune, India.
      Services provided: 30 civic services to citizens and local establishments from birth and death certificates, to shop licenses, property tax payments and water bill payments, among several others.
      Authority: Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC)
      Business Hours: 10:00 hrs to 18:00 hrs (Monday to Saturday)
      Call: 1800 10 30222
      Email: info@punecorporation.org
      Visit: www.pmc.gov.in
