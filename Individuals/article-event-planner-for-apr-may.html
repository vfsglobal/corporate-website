• Tanzcafe Arlberg, Austria

      April 1 - 18

      Tanzcafe Arlberg festival – a new fixture on the Austrian ski scene’s calendar – is an homage to the region’s Twenties boom years, which saw the small town of Lech overrun by Jazz bands and Swing orchestras.

    • Songkran, Thailand

      April 13 - 15

      In mid-April, Thais celebrate their own New Year called Songkran. Traditionally, people return to their families and pay respect to the elders by pouring a little scented water on their hands. This is also the opportunity of huge water fights in the street.

    • Coachella, California, USA

      April 19 - 21

      The first major music event in the annual festival calendar, the line-up for this year includes artists like Khalid, J Balvin, Bad Bunny, Wiz Khalifa, Childish Gambino and Ariana Grande.

    • Summer Season 2019, London, UK

      April 23 - October 13

      Fill long sunny days with some of Shakespeare’s most acclaimed plays as the world-famous Shakespeare’s Globe stages its Summer Season 2019.

    • Frieze Art Fair, New York, USA

      May 2 - 5

      A highlight of the year for the culturally minded, the Frieze Art Fair in New York showcases works by more than 1,000 contemporary artists. This is your chance to experience a curated program of talks, performances and new artist commissions.

    • Antar and Abla, Muscat, Oman

      May 3 - 4

      Based on a fabled tale from the Arab mediaeval era, the balladic opera will be performed with a fabulous cast of solo and choral singers, dancers and musicians. In times of conflict with dangerous enemies, the heroic Antar triumphs both in battle and love.

    • Hay Festival, United Kingdom

      May 23 - June 2

      This is the world’s largest philosophy festival. Famous thinkers, authors and other cultural figures will discuss, debate and postulate deep-rooted topics.

    • The White Nights Festival, St. Petersburg, Russia

      May 23 - June 2

      The White Nights Festival is held at the city’s famous Mariinsky Theatre and showcases some of the world’s best opera and ballet.

    • Distortion, Copenhagen, Denmark

      May 29 - June 2

      The party-culture festival attracts DJs from across the globe and sees the city’s streets overwhelmed with thousands of revellers over its five-day span. Free street parties take place each day in a different district.

    • ICC Cricket World Cup, England and Wales

      May 30 onwards

      The 2019 Cricket World Cup is the 12th edition of the Cricket World Cup, scheduled to be hosted by England and Wales. Ten top teams from the world will compete for the coveted cup.
