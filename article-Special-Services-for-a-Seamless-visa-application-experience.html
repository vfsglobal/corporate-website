    Premium Courier Service

    For all UK visa applicants, VFS Global, in collaboration with UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI), has launched an optional service for UK visa applicants in India – the Premium Courier 500 – that provides an additional cover for the processed passports and documents being returned via courier. The service insures the travel documents to the value of US$500, providing compensation in case of any loss, theft or damage of the passport or documents during transit.
