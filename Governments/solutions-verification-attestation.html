Leveraging its vast global reach and rich in-country knowledge, VFS Global understands the vital need for a well-networked, scalable and professionally managed Verification and Attestation procedures to authenticate the information and supporting documents submitted during the application and eligibility process. With the establishment of dedicated units across the globe, the company delivers robust end-to-end services in this critical area of decision-making through document and information authentication.

VFS Global’s Verification and Attestation Services can be uniquely facilitated as independent services or in conjunction with each other to deliver an end-to-end unified process. Services are aimed at individuals or corporate entities that need to have educational, personal and commercial documents legally recognised to meet the statutory requirements for processing various travel- or migration-related applications.
