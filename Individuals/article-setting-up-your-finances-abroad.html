• Taking control of your finances is one of the key requirements while you draw plans to move overseas. Whether you are moving to London, Beijing or Sydney, you can take proactive steps to make sure that finances are in place before you arrive at a foreign land.

    • Open an account before you arrive

      In some instances, it is possible to set up a bank account before you arrive. This can be either an international bank account that enables you to access multiple currencies, or a bank account to which you can apply online and then go into a branch when you land to verify your identity.

      Having an account set up allows you to transfer money so that it is available for you when you arrive.

    • Consider what you need

      Before you leave, you may want to take a relook at your current financial situation. You may want to surrender a credit card that may no longer be required or close a bank account for similar reasons.

      Once you arrive, evaluate your financial requirements. The rules, regulations and requirements vary from one country to another. Therefore, one must do some research and study before opting for a financial product or service.

    • Select the best way to transfer

      Deciding on the amount you need to transfer and the frequency of transfer may help you choose the best options. How much and how often you need to transfer may determine the best way for you to move your money.
