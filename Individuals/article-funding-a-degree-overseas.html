• Overseas Education has many benefits, but it usually comes with high costs and the need to manage money across countries.

      There may be additional costs on top of accommodation & tuitions fees in form of flight expenses to visit your family. In order to obtain a visa, international students also need to prove they have enough money to support themselves and pay for their course.

    • Ways to fund education overseas

      It may be possible to work while studying in the UK – subject to the conditions of your visa – but here are some other ways to cover the costs.

    • Savings

      If you don’t already have a clear idea of how you will fund a degree overseas, it’s never too early to start planning. An international account could be a place to start, particularly if you can get into the habit of making regular contributions.

    • Investing

      If you’re preparing to fund your child’s degree and have several years in hand , you might want to consider international investments. However, while offering the potential for higher returns, an investment carries certain risks and you may end up with losses.

      It is also important to set money aside for emergencies before you consider investing.
