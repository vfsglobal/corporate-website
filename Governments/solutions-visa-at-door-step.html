This service is intuitively designed to offer customers an unmatched convenience when submitting visa applications. Supported by the company’s innovative Mobile Biometrics solution, a team of trained staff visit individual or group applicants at a location of their choice to enrol their biometrics and collect their entire visa application.

This service is particularly ideal for large groups and families, as the entire applicant group can enroll their biometrics and submit their applications at the same time and place, without the hassle of having to travel to a visa application centre.
