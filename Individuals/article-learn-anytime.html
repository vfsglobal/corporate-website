• Knowledge is like the proverbial elephant; each learner approaches it in a different way. Learning has evolved beyond the classroom, and tech legends such as Steve Jobs, Bill Gates and Mark Zuckerberg (and several other dropout-millionaires) show that there’s more than a single way to learn. Whether you were a star pupil or always at the back of the class, teaching theory now looks to recognise each person for what they are worth – each a “different kind of learner” with a different set of talents and a different method of articulation.

    Keeping pace with this evolving view of teaching and learning, VFS Global Edu Support Services Pvt. Ltd. has launched ‘VFS-Oxademy’, a new digital learning platform in partnership with UK-based Edtech Oxademy Technologies and its subsidiary, the Oxademy Business School.

    The online-guided learning platform, a first-of-its-kind worldwide, currently offers PG and Executive Education Programmes in four fields of business administration and leadership studies. The courses offered are:

    • MBA: Master of Business Administration
    • MSL: Masters in Strategic Leadership
    • MSOL: Masters of Science in Organisational Leadership
    • GLDP: Global Leadership Development Programme (4-6 months professional certificate programme)

    The study programmes are designed in partnership with distinguished US and UK-based universities and institutions, and include classes and lectures by top-rated faculty members and guest speakers.

    The unique feature of VFS-Oxademy’s online modules is that it uses OX360, the award-winning Artificial Intelligence (AI) cloud infrastructure/technology that is sensitive to the specific behaviour and learning patterns of each individual learner. According to the personalised learning pathway rolled out for the individual learner, he/she can finish the degree course in a period of 16, 24 or 36 months.

    As an added benefit, the OX360 Mobile App also facilitates students to learn the concepts included in their curricula in convenient, bite-sized portions. It also enables students to receive alerts about upcoming study topics and to connect with their course tutors.

    Busy executives wishing to advance their job prospects by bettering their educational qualification can conveniently log on to the VFS-Oxademy study programmes after office hours and earn their degrees in due course, all without taking leave from work.

    The entry requirements for enroling for the study programmes include:

    • A minimum of a lower second-class honours degree or equivalent qualification
    • A minimum of two years relevant work experience, which includes managerial responsibility (work experience is a must for MBA)
    • English proficiency test (IELTS 6 or equivalent)

    The programmes are delivered either in a fully online mode or as a mix of virtual learning and real-world classroom-learning. In tandem with the programmes, there is always a real-time analysis of a learner’s performance aswell.

    These heavyweight, accredited VFS-Oxademy degrees are on par with and identical to on-campus courses, but are lighter on the pocket as they cost significantly less than a traditional course. Nevertheless, students benefit from all the tools and facilities that are accessible to on-campus students.

    VFS-Oxademy students also have access to the world’s largest digital library (containing more than 1 million ebooks) as well as an internationally-recognised platform for publishing their business research projects. They can additionally benefit from the prospect of working on real-world projects with an Oxademy Industry Partner (OIP).

    Upon completion of the course, students can attend their graduation ceremony in the UK or USA.
